Villa FourTuna

A modern aparthotel on the shores of the Adriatic Sea awaits guests.

About us

The aparthotel is a cozy villa located in a quiet area of the city of Bar, Montenegro.

The doors of our hotel have been open to visitors since 2015.

Over this time, we have received a large number of positive reviews and consistently maintain a high rating on Booking.com.

Villa FourTuna comprises six comfortable rooms. On the villa's premises, there is a convenient heated pool equipped with a retractable roof, allowing its use in any weather.

For our young guests, there is an outdoor playground as well as an indoor playroom. The parking accommodates up to 7 cars.
